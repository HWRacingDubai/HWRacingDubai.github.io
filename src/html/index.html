What is HWRacing Dubai?

We are HWRacing, Heriot-Watt University's formula student team. Each year we design and build a single-seat open-wheel racing car and enter it into the formula student competition at Silverstone.

about us

Formula Student

15 members from our team head to the Silverstone Circuit from the 17th to the 21st of July, 2024, to compete in Formula Student's concept class to showcase the concept of 2025's challenger, where we will go head-to-head against some of the best university teams in the world.
